Chicken Biryani

A royal Indian delicacy that combines aromatic basmati rice with tender, spiced chicken, slow-cooked to perfection. Our Chicken Biryani is a symphony of flavors that tells a story of culinary tradition and passion.

Spice Level

Medium

Cooking Time

60-75 minutes

Serving Size

4-6 persons

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (bone-in, cut into pieces)
  • 2 cups basmati rice (soaked for 30 minutes)
  • 1 large onion (thinly sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 green cardamoms
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 3-4 cloves
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1/4 cup milk (with saffron soaked)
  • 2 tbsp mint leaves (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves (chopped)
  • Salt to taste

Cooking Process

Step 1: Marinate the Chicken

Mix chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 1 hour.

Step 2: Cook the Rice

Boil water with bay leaf, cardamoms, cinnamon, and salt. Add soaked rice and cook until 80% done. Drain and keep aside.

Step 3: Prepare the Masala

Heat ghee/oil in a pan, fry onions until golden brown. Add tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste, and spices. Cook until oil separates.

Step 4: Cook the Chicken

Add marinated chicken to the masala and cook for 15-20 minutes until tender.

Step 5: Layering the Biryani

Layer half the cooked rice over the chicken. Add fried onions, mint, coriander, and saffron milk. Repeat layers.

Step 6: Dum Cooking

Cover tightly and cook on low heat for 20 minutes. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.

Enjoy Your Chicken Biryani! 🍛
